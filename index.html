 Tips to Increase Your Privacy and Security on The Internet

Common Password

Make sure your password is unique and something you would type. Commonly used passwords are very easy to crack.

Too Short

For extra security, make sure your password has atleast 8 characters.

Names and Words

Although they are easy to remember, personal names and relevant increases the chances of having your account compromised.

Symbols and Numbers

Add some variety to your password by using more than just one letters. Symbols and numbers will help to make your password more secure.

Secure Your Data

Did you know that most online services, in their Terms and Conditions, reserve the right to reproduce and distribute your data? No matter how strong your password may be, chances are you have granted access to your personal information already. From now on, read the T&C carefully before agreeing!
